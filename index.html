Projects

Tech Intiatives

NeuroHarmony

NeuroHarmony is an innovative app designed to improve focus, mental wellness, and cognitive health for seniors, including those living with Alzheimer’s and other memory-related challenges. By using personalized music and brain exercises grounded in neuroscience, the app provides a calming, stimulating, and meaningful experience that supports mental clarity, memory retention, and emotional well-being. NeuroHarmony tracks progress over time, adapting exercises and playlists to maximize cognitive benefits, helping seniors stay engaged, mentally active, and emotionally uplifted. With its intuitive interface and engaging visuals, NeuroHarmony transforms daily mindfulness and mental training into an enjoyable, empowering routine, enhancing the quality of life for older adults and supporting their overall mental health.

Soarin' Around the World

Soarin’ Around the World is an immersive travel app designed to bring the joy of exploration to seniors, including those with limited mobility or cognitive challenges. By combining virtual reality technology with interactive maps, users can virtually visit iconic landmarks, scenic destinations, and cultural hotspots from around the globe—all from the comfort of their own home. The app provides detailed 3D tours, engaging educational content, and curated travel experiences that stimulate the mind, spark curiosity, and encourage mental activity. For seniors with Alzheimer’s or other memory concerns, Soarin’ Around the World offers a safe and uplifting way to explore new places, reminisce about familiar ones, and enhance overall cognitive health and emotional well-being.
